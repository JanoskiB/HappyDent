Dr. Lehoczky Márton

Specialist in oral surgery

I obtained my dental degree in 2019 at the University of Szeged and in December 2022, I became a specialist in dento-alveolar oral surgery. In addition to my private practice, I am involved in patient care, research, and student education at the Department of Oral Surgery of the Faculty of Dentistry of SZTE. In addition to oral surgery, I work with aesthetic and biomimetic dentistry, which deals with the near-natural restoration of severely weakened teeth, is very close to my heart. I spend my free time reading and playing sports.
