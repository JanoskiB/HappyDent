Relax and let us take care of you!

We are Lehoczky Dental, a Hungarian dental clinic with more than 30 years of experience. In our practice, each doctor and assistant strives to find the most suitable and long-term solutions for your problem, based on your possibilities and needs.

What can you expect at our clinic?

Completely pain-free treatment

Thanks to the most modern procedures, all treatments in our office are completely painless, so that your dental treatment can remain a positive experience.

Modern and digitalized equipment

We have everything for the perfect result. We can implement the needs of our patients as quickly and precisely as possible.

Thorough, all-inclusive dental care

Our specialists are constantly training themselves to bring you the best of modern dental procedures. We offer solutions for all your dental problems.

Meet our staff

Our colleagues are professionals who are eager to help you achieve the smile of your dreams!

Dr. Lehoczky Márton

I obtained my dental degree in 2019 at the University of Szeged. Already when I was a student, it became clear to me that this profession, which I had chosen, is an area that is developing at an extraordinary pace, therefore, after obtaining my diploma..., I decided that I would like to take a professional exam. I can call myself lucky that my plan was realized: in December 2022, I became a specialist in dento-alveolar oral surgery. Currently, in addition to my private practice, I am involved in patient care, research, and student education at the Department of Oral Surgery of the Faculty of Dentistry of SZTE. In addition to oral surgery, aesthetic and biomimetic dentistry, which deals with the near-natural restoration of severely weakened teeth, is very close to my heart. I spend my free time reading and playing sports.
