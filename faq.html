Consultation meeting

How much does a consultation meeting cost?

The consultation meeting is free, although before the consultation you must provide the full jaw CBCT scan which cost depends on your home country dentrist's prices.

How long does a consultation meeting last?

The length of the consultation meeting depends on the severity of your problem and the amount of questions you might have about the process. In general meetings last around 15-30 minutes.

How can I set up a consultation meeting?

To set up a video consultation meeting with our staff, you have to contact us, by filling out details about yourself and your dental issue.

Treatments

What kind of treatments do you offer?

The list of treatments can be seen on our Treatments page, which includes a brief explanation of each treatment.

How long will the healing period last?

Healing period depends on a specific treatment and the severity of your issue. When you book a meeting with our staff, you are encouraged to ask about the healing period for your specific case.

What if the treatment I am looking for is not on the list?

Our clinic provides all round dental service, so in case the treatment you are interested in is not on our list, you are encouraged to contact the clinic with questions regarding that.
