What are the steps of the dental tourism process?

Setting up a consultation meeting

To understand your dental problems, and further inform you about the process, we hold a consultation meeting.

Before the meeting

In order to set up a consultation meeting, it is important for you to provide the clinic with a full jaw CBCT scan done in your home country. The CBCT scan will privide our staff with the basic information about the condition of your teeth and help evaluate the size of your issue. After you get your CBCT scan, you can book the time for the meeting and attach the scan to your booking. Our staff will get back to you as soon as possible with the confirmation that the meeting is booked, and provide you with all the neccessary information.

During the meeting

During your meeting our staff will interview you about your issue and discuss possible treatments. In case of any questions about the treatments and/or the cost of them, feel free to ask our staff.

After the meeting

After the meeting you will recieve an offer for possible times for your treatment, as well as an instruction in case the treatment requires any special preparations. When you accept the date and time for you rtreatment, you will get another confirmation email. From this point you can start planning your trip to Hungary.

Planning your vacation

We present you solutions for every step of your journey, so you will never find yourself lost in a foreign country.

Choose your flight

When you confirm the time for your treatment and contact out staff, we will help you with finding best possible option for the airport near you and the flight tickets.

Arranging transfer from the airport to the hotel

You can always choose your own way of transportation, but if you would like, our staff can order a transfer service for you that will meet you in the airport and take you right to your accomodation.

Finding accommodation close to our clinic

There are many beautiful accomodations in Szeged to choose from. Along with the information about the flights, we will provide with a selection of accomodations that are close to the clinic and the city center, as it takes only 10 mins by bus between the two.

Spending your holiday and getting treatment in Hungary

We will stay in close contact with you to make sure everything goes according to the plan.

Arriving in Hungary

Visiting the clinic

The day before your appointment you will get a notification with a reminder. It is a good idea to come to the clinic 10 minutes before your treatment in order to register and get acquainted with our staff.
